snowballing

find the one relevant article

backward snowballing
  - find all citations that paper used to build their case

forward snowballing
  - find all papers where that paper was cited


precision
  - (true positives / selected elements)

recall
  - (true positives / relevant elements)

F1 score
  - the harmonic mean of the precision and recall. 
  - 2 * ((precision * recall) / (precision + recall))
